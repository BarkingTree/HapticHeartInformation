Haptic Heart

Utilizing Haptic Feedback as a Cardiovascular Auscultation Training Tool

Haptic Heart: A novel haptic cardiac simulator designed to help medical students and junior resident doctors practice their cardiovascular examination techniques! Haptic Heart generates patterns of vibrations designed to mimic the human heartbeat. It can generate additional heart sounds, murmurs or even a combination of both. You can even practice at different heart rates and see how this changes the pattern, adjust the intensity of different elements within the pattern and use the in-built quiz function to help you test your knowledge.
